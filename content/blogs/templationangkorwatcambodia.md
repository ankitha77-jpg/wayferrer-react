### **Why We Chose Templation in Cambodia**

After a few budget stays in Vietnam, I wanted to treat myself during our **Cambodia** leg—and **Templation by MAADS** was just what we needed. This **5-star boutique property** near Angkor Wat blends luxury, nature, and Khmer culture, making it the perfect balance between relaxation and heritage.


![Blog Image](/blog-images/templationangkorwatcambodia-1.jpg)


### **First Impressions & Check-In Experience**

A quick 20-minute drive from the airport brought us to this tranquil oasis. We were greeted with **cold towels, welcome drinks**, and a sweet treat while the staff prepped our room.

Covid safety was fully in place, and the staff's **kindness and care** stood out—especially when they checked in on me after I sprained my ankle.


![Blog Image](/blog-images/templationangkorwatcambodia-2.jpg)


![Blog Image](/blog-images/templationangkorwatcambodia-4.jpg)

The staff were so courteous, friendly and thoughtful; I sprained my leg during a hike and they kept checking on me to make sure I had everything I needed to rest my ankle. Our minds were at ease from the second we set foot here. 


![Blog Image](/blog-images/templationangkorwatcambodia-6.jpg)


### **Location: 10 Minutes from Angkor Wat**

The resort's **proximity to Angkor Wat** was a game-changer. We woke at 4:45 a.m. and reached the temples by 5:00 a.m., skipping the long drives others endure. Trust me, with Cambodia’s heat, being close by matters!


![Blog Image](/blog-images/templationangkorwatcambodia-7.jpg)


### **Sustainability Practices**

Templation is part of the **MAADS group**, known for sustainability and supporting local communities. Eco-conscious decor, local staff, and minimal environmental footprint make it an ethical luxury option.

### **Final Thoughts**

Templation offers a **private luxury experience** that feels authentic and sustainable. Its peaceful ambiance, exceptional staff, and eco-friendly design made our Cambodia trip extra memorable.


![Blog Image](/blog-images/templationangkorwatcambodia-68.jpg)

The whole resort had a zen vibe about it. It was fantastic way for us to escape the tourist crowds at the temple, yet it was at just the right distance from the city center. 


![Blog Image](/blog-images/templationangkorwatcambodia-69.jpg)


![Blog Image](/blog-images/cambodia-travel-angkor-wat-itinerary-guide-17.jpg)

**Pro tip:** Stay **at least 3 nights** to truly unwind and explore. We left feeling rested, cared for, and fully immersed in the Cambodian charm.
